Anime is a style of animation that originated in Japan and has become popular worldwide. It encompasses a wide range of genres, including action, adventure, romance, fantasy, science fiction, horror, and more. Anime often features colorful artwork, vibrant characters, intricate storylines, and diverse themes that appeal to audiences of all ages.
